This summary will only be read by you on the next turn — write for an LLM, not for a human. Be dense, structured, and information-preserving. Do not introduce yourself or write a closing.

8. **Current Work** — What was being worked on at the moment the summary was triggered.
9. **Next Step** — Only include if it directly continues the user's most recent instruction.
