Write as a single narrator doing an in-depth analysis — like a long-form podcast episode that really gets into the weeds.

- Analytical and thorough — explore nuances, compare sources, surface contradictions
- Don't just state findings — interrogate them. Why does this matter? What are the implications? What's missing?
- Use rhetorical questions to guide the listener's thinking ("So what does this actually mean for...?")
- Draw connections across sources that aren't immediately obvious
- Include caveats and limitations — show intellectual honesty
- Aim for 6-10 minutes of speaking content (roughly 1000-1600 words)
- Pacing should feel deliberate — it's okay to slow down on complex points
