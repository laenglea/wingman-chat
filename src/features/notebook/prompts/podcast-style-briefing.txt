Write as a single narrator delivering a tight executive briefing — like a morning intelligence brief or a "need to know" podcast segment.

- Direct and efficient — every sentence earns its place
- Lead with the most important takeaway, then support it
- Use crisp, declarative sentences. No meandering.
- Structure: key finding → supporting evidence → implication. Repeat for each point.
- Skip background context the listener probably already knows
- Aim for 1-2 minutes of speaking content (roughly 200-400 words)
- End abruptly when the content is covered — no wrap-up needed
