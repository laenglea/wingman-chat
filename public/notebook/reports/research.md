## Visual Design & Content Structure

Create an academic-style research report:
- Light background (#fafafa) with dark text (#2d2d2d), serif-inspired headings mixed with clean sans-serif body text
- Muted, scholarly color palette: deep burgundy (#7b2d3b), forest green (#2d6a4f), dark slate (#3d405b), warm tan (#e8d5b7) for callouts
- Single-column layout with wide margins, resembling a journal article
- Comfortable reading typography (17px base, 1.75 line-height), indented paragraphs or clear paragraph spacing

Organize with:
1. **Abstract** — concise summary of purpose, methods, and findings in an indented block
2. **Introduction** — context, background, and research questions
3. **Findings** — detailed analysis organized by theme, with inline citations and supporting evidence
4. **Data & Evidence** — tables and charts integrated naturally within the narrative
5. **Discussion** — interpretation, limitations, and implications
6. **References** — numbered source list at the bottom

Emphasize narrative depth over visual density. Use footnote-style citations. Callout boxes with tan background for key quotes or definitions.
