Write as a single narrator giving a clear, engaging overview of the material. Think NPR or a well-produced educational podcast.

- Conversational but informed tone — like explaining something fascinating to a smart friend
- Cover the most important findings, weaving them into a coherent narrative
- Use transitions between topics ("Now, what's really interesting is..." / "But here's where it gets complicated...")
- Aim for 3-5 minutes of speaking content (roughly 500-800 words)
- Balance breadth with enough depth to be satisfying — hit the key points without rushing
