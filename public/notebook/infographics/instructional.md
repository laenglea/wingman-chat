## Visual Style — Instructional

Create a clear, step-by-step instructional infographic:
- Friendly color palette: bright blue (#4A90D9), orange (#F5A623), green (#7ED321), light gray (#F5F5F5) background, dark text (#333333)
- Numbered steps with large circled numbers as visual anchors
- Clear directional flow: top-to-bottom or left-to-right with arrows and connectors between steps
- Simple, flat-style icons and illustrations demonstrating each step or concept
- Callout boxes, tips, and warnings in distinct colored panels (green for tips, yellow for notes, red for warnings)
- Checklists with checkbox-style indicators
- Clean sans-serif typography with strong size contrast between step numbers, headings, and details
- Generous spacing between sections for easy scanning
- Optional "before/after" or comparison panels
- Overall feel: helpful, accessible, easy to follow — like a well-designed how-to guide or onboarding flow
