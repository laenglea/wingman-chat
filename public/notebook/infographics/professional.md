## Visual Style — Professional

Create a clean, corporate professional infographic:
- Sophisticated color palette: navy (#0B2545), steel blue (#3E6D9C), slate gray (#6C757D), white, with gold (#C9A227) or teal (#2EC4B6) accent
- Clean sans-serif typography with strong hierarchy — large bold headings, medium subheads, refined body text
- Precise geometric layouts with clear grid alignment and generous whitespace
- Polished data visualizations: clean bar charts, donut charts, line graphs with smooth curves
- Icon style: thin-line professional icons, minimal and consistent
- Section dividers: subtle horizontal rules or color-blocked regions
- Key metrics displayed in large bold numbers with small descriptive labels
- Overall feel: authoritative, trustworthy, boardroom-ready — suitable for executive presentations and reports
