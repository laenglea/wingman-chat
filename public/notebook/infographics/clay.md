## Visual Style — Clay

Create a 3D claymation/clay-style infographic:
- Warm, tactile color palette: terracotta (#E07A5F), sage green (#81B29A), dusty blue (#3D7EA6), cream (#F2E9DC), warm brown (#8B6A4E)
- All elements look like they're made of soft clay or plasticine — rounded, slightly lumpy, organic shapes
- 3D rendered appearance with soft shadows and ambient occlusion
- Smooth matte surfaces with subtle fingerprint-like texture
- Icons and illustrations modeled as miniature clay sculptures
- Rounded typography that looks embossed or pressed into clay
- Sections separated by clay-like ridges, borders, or extruded shapes
- Data visualizations as physical clay objects: stacked clay discs for bar charts, clay spheres for bubbles
- Soft, diffused studio lighting with gentle gradients
- Overall feel: playful, handcrafted, tangible — like a stop-motion animation set piece
