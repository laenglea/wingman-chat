## Visual Style — Bento Grid

Create a bento box grid-style infographic:
- Modern color palette: white (#FFFFFF) cards on a soft gray (#F0F0F0) or dark (#1A1A2E) background, with one vibrant accent color per card section
- Bento-box layout: content organized in a tight asymmetric grid of rounded-rectangle cards of varying sizes — some tall, some wide, some square
- Each card contains one focused piece of information: a stat, a chart, an icon, or a short text block
- Large cards for hero stats or main visuals, small cards for supporting details
- Subtle card shadows or borders with generous corner radius (16-24px)
- Clean, modern sans-serif typography — bold large numbers, minimal text
- Flat-style or subtle gradient icons within cards
- No visual clutter within cards — one idea per cell, maximum breathing room
- Cards can have distinct background colors from a harmonious palette
- Overall feel: modern, modular, app-like — inspired by Apple's UI design, Notion dashboards, and bento-style social media posts
