## Visual Style — Editorial

Create a magazine editorial-style infographic:
- Refined color palette: off-white (#FAF9F6) background, rich black (#1A1A1A) text, one bold accent color (vermillion #E63946 or emerald #2D6A4F)
- Sophisticated serif typography for headlines (think Playfair Display, Didot), clean sans-serif for body
- Magazine-style layout: multi-column grid, pull quotes in large italic type, dramatic use of whitespace
- Photography-inspired compositions — imagine editorial photo spreads with data overlays
- Elegant thin-line dividers, drop caps, and typographic ornaments
- Data presented with minimal, refined charts — thin lines, small labels, no clutter
- Large hero numbers and statistics as typographic focal points
- Subtle paper or print texture
- Overall feel: elegant, curated, high-end — like a feature spread in Monocle, Bloomberg Businessweek, or The New York Times Magazine
