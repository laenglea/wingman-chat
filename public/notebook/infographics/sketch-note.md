## Visual Style — Sketch Note

Create a hand-drawn sketch-note style infographic:
- Looks like it was drawn by hand on paper or a whiteboard with markers and pens
- Wobbly lines, imperfect shapes, hand-lettered typography with varying weight
- Multi-color palette: warm orange, teal, navy, coral, and leaf green on a white or cream background
- Doodle-style icons and illustrations — lightbulbs, arrows, speech bubbles, stars, banners
- Connecting elements: hand-drawn arrows, dotted lines, curved paths linking sections
- Mixed layout: content flows organically, not rigidly aligned — some elements tilted or circled
- Emphasis through thick underlines, highlight boxes, and exclamation marks
- Charts and data visualizations in sketchy hand-drawn style with rough shading
- Overall feel: creative, personal, educational — like a visual thinker's notebook
