## Visual Style — Kawaii

Create a cute, kawaii-style infographic:
- Soft pastel color palette: baby pink (#FFB5C2), lavender (#C3B1E1), mint (#A8E6CF), butter yellow (#FFEAA7), sky blue (#A0D2DB)
- Rounded, bubbly shapes with thick soft outlines
- Cute character mascots and faces on objects — smiling clouds, happy stars, blushing food items
- Decorative elements: sparkles, hearts, tiny flowers, rainbow accents, confetti dots
- Rounded sans-serif or handwritten bubbly typography
- Soft drop shadows and subtle gradients for depth
- Sections framed in rounded rectangles or cloud shapes
- Data visualizations with cute icons instead of plain bars or lines
- Overall feel: cheerful, approachable, playful — like a Japanese stationery design
